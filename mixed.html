Mixed typesÂ¶

PyOP2 supports combining objects of type Set, DataSet, Dat and Map to form coupled or mixed types as they are required e.g. in finite-element methods to solve coupled system of equations. This is the basis for the support of mixed spaces in Firedrake.

Mixed objects are created by passing an iterable (a list, tuple or a finite generator) of the objects to combine into a mixed object into the corresponding mixed type constructor. All mixed objects are iterable, yielding their constituent objects, and support indexing in the usual Python syntax, with the exception of Map, where indexing creates an indexed Map.
